Our Founder

"Every rep. Every drop of sweat. Every transformation — this is why Evolve 360° exists."

Evolve 360° was born from a singular vision — to create a fitness space in Chennai that makes elite training accessible to everyone in the community. For over 5 years, we have been transforming lives, one workout at a time.

With a completed Fitness Coaching certification and a deep passion for helping people stay fit and healthy, our founder built this studio as a sanctuary for anyone who wants to level up — physically and mentally.

The mission is simple: help the surrounding community live healthier, stronger, and more confident lives — and Evolve 360° is the home where that mission comes alive every single day.
